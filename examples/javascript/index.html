ImageKit Video Player Example

This example demonstrates how to use the ImageKit Video Player with a playlist. The player is initialized with a video and a playlist of products.

The player supports various actions such as seeking to a specific time in the video, switching images on hover, and navigating to product pages on click.

The player is designed to be responsive and works well on different screen sizes. You can customize the player styles and behavior as needed.
